    Sponsorship

    We are seeking sponsors to help offset the costs of running the conference. Sponsorship allows us to keep ticket prices low.

    Bronze sponsors receive:

    • Logo link on the sidebar of this website, with bronze placement
    • Opportunity to submit 1 flyer/brochure per giveaway bag
    • Opportunity to submit 1 promo item for the giveaway bags
    • 1 ticket to PyCon Philippines
    • Mention in the welcome and closing remarks as a bronze sponsor
    • Bronze placement on banners at conference

    Silver sponsors receive:

    • Logo link on the sidebar of this website, with silver placement
    • Opportunity to submit up to 2 flyers/brochures per giveaway bag
    • Opportunity to submit up to 2 promo items for the giveaway bags
    • 200-character text description of their company on website
    • Opportunity to organize a party (breakfast or evening social) at PyCon Philippines
    • 2 tickets to PyCon Philippines
    • Mention in the welcome and closing remarks as a silver sponsor
    • Silver placement on banners at conference

    Gold sponsors receive:

    • Logo link on the sidebar of this website, with gold placement
    • Opportunity to submit up to 3 flyers/brochures per giveaway bag
    • Opportunity to submit up to 3 promo items for the giveaway bags
    • 400-character text description of their company on website
    • Opportunity to organize a party (breakfast or evening social) at PyCon Philippines
    • 3 tickets to PyCon Philippines
    • Mention in the welcome and closing remarks as a gold sponsor
    • Gold placement on banners at conference

    Questions? Contact Daniel Greenfeld: pydanny (at) gmail {dot} com.
