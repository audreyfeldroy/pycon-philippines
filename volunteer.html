    Call for volunteers

    Want to volunteer to help? Go to the the PinoyPUG Facebook group and offer to help with one of the following:

    • Marketing Chair: Sends emails to the various university engineering and science departments advertising the conference. Must have contacts with at least 1 university.
    • Food/Catering Chair: Coordinates lunch, coffee, snacks, and dinner catering. Experience with event planning/catering is needed.
    • A/V Chair: Coordinates all audio/video matters with Frank and the conference venue. Must be willing to bring in a/v equipment if needed, or must have a connection to someone who can help.

    If you have other ideas about tasks that you can do to make this a better conference, suggest them here.
