    Volunteers

    Frank Pohlmann - Conference Chair

    Frank is the PyCon Philippines conference chair and the main person in charge. He's coordinating the venue logistics, talks, and basically everything else not covered by anyone. You can email him at pycon (at) auberonsolutions {dot} com.

    Ann Tan-Pohlman and Melanie P. Diosana - Logistics Co-Chairs

    Ann and Melanie have put countless hours into coordinating the logistics for PyCon Philippines, including volunteer coordination. You can reach them via the Philippine Python Facebook group. In particular, look for the calls for volunteers, because they can always use more help.

    Mark Steve Samson - Design Chair

    Mark is the person behind the design of the PHPUG and PyCon Philippines logo and t-shirt. You can reach him via Twitter (@themarksteve).

    Daniel Greenfeld - Sponsorship and Community Relations Chair

    If you have any community relations or sponsorship questions, please email Daniel at pydanny (at) cartwheelweb {dot} com or via Twitter (@pydanny).

    Audrey Roy - Website Chair

    Audrey is the maintainer of the PyCon Philippines website. If you'd like to help, simply submit a pull request to with your changes. You can email her at audreyr (at) cartwheelweb {dot} com, or message her via Twitter (@audreyr).

    Call for volunteers

    Want to volunteer to help? There is an open call for volunteers on the Philippine Python Facebook group. 4 hours of volunteer work is equivalent to a ticket to the conference.
