    Register for PyCon Philippines

    Registration is now open. We have tried our best to keep ticket prices low, to make the conference accessible to as many people as possible.

    Registration includes:

    • Admission to a full day of technical talks on Saturday
    • Lunch on Saturday
    • Admission to an evening social on Saturday night
    • Admission to a session of technical talks on Sunday morning
    • Admission to a full day of sprints on Sunday
    • Lunch on Sunday
    • PyCon Philippines t-shirt
    • PyCon Philippines goodie bag with giveaways from sponsors

    Financial aid: if you cannot afford your ticket, send an email to audreyr (at) cartwheelweb {dot} com describing your situation. We strive to ensure that a diverse audience can attend, including those who are unemployed, under-employed, students, or in need for other reasons.

    Register before June 15, 2012 for an early bird discount. (Regular registration will be ₱395 regular / ₱350 student / ₱795 corporate.)
